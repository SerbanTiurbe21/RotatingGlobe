Some simple instructions to get you started:

  • Install the Vite CLI.
  • Run vite to start the dev server.
  • Open index.html in your browser.
  • Use the mouse to drag the sphere in order to rotate it.
  • Press P to stop the rotation of the sphere.
  • Hover over the blue dots to see the country's name and population.
  • Press E in order to increase the rotation speed of the sphere and Q to decrease it.
